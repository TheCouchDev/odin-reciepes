Homemade Barbacoa Tacos!

This is a simple yet delicious version of barbacoa tacos. Barbacoa is an authentic Mexican dish that typically uses beef or lamb that is slowly cooked until tender and shredded for tacos.

INGREDIENTS LIST:

  • 4lbs Short Ribs
  • 2 cups (475 mL) Beef Stock
  • 8 Garlic Cloves (Peeled and Smashed)
  • 4 Guajillo Chiles
  • 2 tsp (5 g) Ground Cumin
  • 3 Bay Leaves
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • Salt

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Cut Meat into 3-inch chunks and season with salt
  2. Heat a heavy-bottomed pot over medium-high heat. Add vegetable oil just to coat the bottom.
  3. Add short ribs in batches. Sear 2-3 minutes per side making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Set to the side and repeat with the remaining pieces.
  4. Turn off the heat and add meat chunks back to the pot. Add in beef stock, garlic cloves, guajillo chiles, ground cumin, bay leaves, and cinnamon stick.
  5. Bring up to a boil over medium-high. Immediately reduce to low and simmer.
  6. Add a parchment paper layer on top followed by the lid.
  7. Place pot in a 300F (149C) preheated oven for 4-6 hours.
  8. Remove from the oven and place in a roasting pan. Finely shred Using forks. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Assemble tacos and garnish with preferred toppings and enjoy!
